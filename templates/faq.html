General Questions
1. How can I join the Rākau Gardening Society?

You can join us by signing up on our website. Choose between an annual or monthly membership plan. Don't forget to check if you're eligible for discounts as a student or community service card holder!

2. What are the benefits of becoming a member?

Members enjoy exclusive access to our workshops, one-on-one lessons with experienced instructors, and our vibrant community forum. Plus, you get to participate in members-only events and receive updates on the latest gardening trends and society news.

3. Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time through your profile settings. Please note that refunds are subject to our terms and conditions.

Workshops and Lessons

Booking is simple! Just log in to your account, browse the available workshops, and select the one you're interested in. You'll find options for different locations, times, and fees to suit your schedule and budget.

Most workshops are designed for a wide range of gardening skills, from beginner to advanced. Specific prerequisites, if any, are listed in the workshop details.

We understand that plans can change. If you're unable to attend, please cancel your booking as soon as possible to free up a spot for other members. While refunds are not typically provided for missed workshops, contact us for specific circumstances.
Advanced Gardening Techniques

We offer specialized workshops focusing on various propagation techniques, including cuttings, layering, and division. Check out our workshop schedule for upcoming sessions on propagation.

Organic fertilizers release nutrients more slowly and improve the structure of the soil over time. Join our sessions on organic gardening to learn more about the benefits and how to make your own compost.

Preventative care is key, but we also cover natural and chemical treatment options in our pest management workshops. Understanding the ecosystem of your garden can help in managing pests and diseases more effectively.
